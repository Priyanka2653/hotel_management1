A Warm Welcome

Enjoy a special meet & greet service at the airport and enhance the experience through our new affordable private transportation. Operated by our trusted service provider, it offers a seamless transfer experience to your chosen destination.

VIP Services

VIP service is about more than a mere luxury. It's about creating once in a lifetime opportunities. At Hotel Elite, we have an impressive list of VIP experiences to make your stay a memorable one. From butler services to dream car hire, we are here to spoil you. All you need to do is ask!

Kids Club

The Kids Club provides attentive care and a host of entertainment options to our young guest. The Kids Club is staffed at all times by professional carers. The service is offered exclusive to in-house guests on a complimentary basis and caters for children aged between 4 to 10 years of age.

Grand Club Lounge

The Grand Club is one of the most exclusive spaces in Hotel Elite and is available to guests who have reserved Grand Club rooms, Grand Club suites, Diplomatic suites and Royal Suites.Open daily from 7:00 a.m. – 11:00 p.m. *

Marquise Boat

Marquise’ Elite’s private boat offers an alternative venue for unique dining and entertainment, while luxuriously cruising the ocean.

Sightseeing

We know your time is precious – so we strive to help you make the most of it at Hotel Elite. Hotel Elite's guests are invited to explore the city through sightseeing tours!!
